Apple Cinnamon Oatmeal

This is a wonderful, simple recipe for an apple cinnamon oatmeal. Even the kids can make this one!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of water
  • 1/4 cup of apple juice
  • 1 apple cored and chopped
  • 2/3 cup rolled oats
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 cup of milk

Steps:

  1. Combine the water, apple juice and apples in a saucepan
  2. Bring to a boil over high heat
  3. Stir in the rolled oats and cinnamon
  4. Return to a boil then reduce heat to low and simmer until thick for about 3 minutes
  5. Spoon into serving bowls and pour milk over the servings
